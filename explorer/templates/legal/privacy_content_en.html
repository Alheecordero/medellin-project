1. General Information

At ViveMedellín (hereinafter "the site", "we" or "our service") we are committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains what information we collect, how we use it and what your rights are regarding it.

ViveMedellín is a directory of places of interest in and around Medellín, including restaurants, bars, cafes and other establishments. By using our site, you accept the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information provided voluntarily

When you use our contact form, you can provide us:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Message or query

2.2 Information collected automatically

When you browse the site, we automatically collect certain technical information:

  • IP address (anonymized when possible)
  • Browser and device type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Approximate location (if you grant permission for the "Near Me" feature)
  • Preferred browser language

2.3 Geolocation data

Our "Near Me" feature uses your location only when you expressly authorize it in your browser. This information is used exclusively to show you nearby places and is not stored on our servers.

We use the information collected to:

  • Show you relevant advertising through Google AdSense
  • Protect the site against fraud and misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

ViveMedellín uses its own and third-party cookies to improve your experience and show personalized advertising.

4.1 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device to remember your preferences and improve your browsing.

4.2 Types of cookies we use

Essential cookies
Necessary for the operation of the site. They include language and security preferences.
Analysis cookies (Google Analytics)
They help us understand how visitors interact with the site. Data is collected on an aggregate and anonymous basis.
Advertising cookies (Google AdSense)
Used to show relevant ads based on your interests. Google and its partners may use cookies to personalize ads.

4.3 Google AdSense and third-party advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertising. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on the user's previous visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.

Important: Users can opt out of Google's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting the Google ad settings or the Network Advertising Initiative.

4.4 How to manage cookies

You can set your browser to reject cookies or alert you when they are being sent. Please note that some features of the site may not function properly without cookies.

  • Manage cookies in Chrome
  • Manage cookies in Firefox
  • Manage cookies in Safari
